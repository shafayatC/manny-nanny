Diversity and Inclusion

All for all — the concept

We take the role of mediation very seriously and offer an open platform for those seeking and offering childcare — regardless of gender, age, origin, culture, sexuality and religion. Differences are an opportunity and an asset for everyone. We do not cling to traditional role models. You notice something, you don't feel addressed or have other suggestions on this topic? Feel free to write us so that we can make our agency as colorful as possible.
