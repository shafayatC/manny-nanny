About us

Placement agency with heart

We want to make families happy — and provide a place for mannies and nannies to find a good job. Reconciling work and children in a relaxed way, care with family-like closeness when everyday family life makes more care necessary at short notice, or simply a short break from children. For this you want the best, kindest and most competent support. We can help and get them to you! Therefore, we have put a lot of effort into our placement process and structured it in a self-explanatory way to make your search as uncomplicated as possible.
