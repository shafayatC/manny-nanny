For mannies and nannies

Childcare — the best job in the world

In childcare, you have the opportunity to do something that is fulfilling and enjoyable — and earn money in a pleasant private work environment. Making everyday life easier for families and enriching it in a positive way, doesn't that sound wonderful? Through our sophisticated system, we bring your wishes together with those of the family. Registration and placement is free for you, so apply now and become part of our team.

How it works

To begin with, we would like to get a first overview and get to know you. Through a simple question-and-answer game we get first key data about you and your job wishes. In order for us to be able to start the placement process more quickly, we ask you to already apply for a detailed leadership certificate at this time.

Once we have confirmed your referral request, you can log into your dashboard. There you can follow all further steps and download e.g. invoices. In the first contact we contact you by phone and arrange an online appointment, where we would like to get to know you better as a family to be able to respond better to your wishes.

The get-to-know-each-other meeting takes place online . You will receive all the necessary information, such as about the placement process, and we will go deeper into your care requests.

With the help of all the data, we go in search of the most suitable childcare. As soon as we have found the perfect manny or nanny for you, we will contact you. Afterwards, you can confirm in your dashboard, so that we can share your contact information.

If the family has also agreed to the match, their contact details will be activated and you can arrange a meeting.

After the meeting, you will then let us know if you are happy with the family so we can finalize the placement. If you decline the match, we will continue the search.

Once the placement has been completed, we are always available to answer any questions you may have.

Amount of fees

Before we start with the placement and find the perfect manny or nanny for you, a nominal fee of €100 is due after the get-to-each-other meeting. Only if it harmonizes personally between you as a family and the care, a placement fee is due. It is calculated by applying a percentage to the annual salary of your care. We will discuss the details together in our get-to-know-each-other meeting.

You are the employers!

The care that suits you will be employed directly by you and will also be paid by you. The level of pay should be commensurate with qualifications. We are available to advise you on salary issues.
