How it works

This is how it works

Our registration is free of charge for you. To find the perfect match for you, we've come up with a simple process that we go through together in a few steps.

To begin with, we would like to get a first overview and get to know you. Through a simple question-and-answer game we get first key data about you and your family.

Once we have confirmed your referral request, you can log into your dashboard. There you can follow all further steps and download e.g. invoices. In the first contact we contact you by phone and arrange an online appointment, where we would like to get to know you better as a family to be able to respond better to your wishes.

The get-to-know-each-other meeting takes place online . You will receive all the necessary information, such as about the placement process, and we will go deeper into your care requests.

With the help of all the data, we go in search of the most suitable childcare. As soon as we have found the perfect manny or nanny for you, we will contact you. Afterwards, you can confirm in your dashboard, so that we can share your contact information.

Once you have agreed to the match, the contact details of the manny or nanny will be unlocked and you can arrange a meeting. After meeting you will then let us know if you are satisfied with your care so that we can finalize placement. If you decline the match, we will continue the search.

Once the placement is complete, we are always available to answer any questions you may have and provide you with information on how to register your care.
